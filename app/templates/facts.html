🌎 About EcoTrack

EcoTrack is a personal sustainability companion designed to help individuals understand, measure, and reduce their environmental impact. Whether you're tracking your transport emissions, energy usage, or food footprint, EcoTrack gives you the tools and insights to make smarter, greener decisions every day.

By combining live environmental news, educational facts, and interactive data, our goal is to empower users to take meaningful action for the planet — one step, one choice, and one fact at a time.

📰 Latest Environmental News

🌿 Trees absorb CO₂

One mature tree can absorb up to 22kg of CO₂ per year, thats roughly 1 ton over 40 years.

The Amazon Rainforest alone stores over 76 billion tonnes of carbon — acting as a global climate buffer.

🚗 Transport Emmissions

Transport accounts for ~25% of global CO₂ emissions.

Switching from car to bike just once a day can reduce a person's transport emissions by 67%.

⚡ Energy Use

LED light bulbs use up to 85% less energy and last 25x longer than traditional incandescent bulbs.

Around 80% of global energy still comes from fossil fuels — coal, oil, and gas — despite the rise of renewables.

🍔Footprint

Producing 1kg of beef emits over 27kg of CO₂, more than driving a petrol car for over 100Km.

A vegan diet can reduce an individual's carbon footprint by up to 50% compared to a meat-based diet.
