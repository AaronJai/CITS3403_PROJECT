Environmental Facts

🌿 Trees absorb CO₂

One mature tree can absorb up to 22kg of CO₂ per year. Planting trees is one of the simplest ways to offset carbon emissions.

🚗 Transport Emissions

Transportation accounts for about 25% of global CO₂ emissions. Walking, biking, or taking public transport helps reduce your footprint.

🍔 Food Footprint

Producing 1kg of beef emits over 27kg of CO₂ — that’s more than driving 100km! Eating more plant-based meals can make a huge impact.

💡 Energy Usage

Turning off unused lights and switching to LEDs can cut your home’s energy use by up to 40%.
